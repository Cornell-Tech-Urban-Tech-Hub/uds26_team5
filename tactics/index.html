Pattern Cards

Use these as diagnostic prompts, not recipes. The same tactic can behave differently when approval stage, local elected alignment, or legal posture changes.

Effectiveness: Low To Medium

Litigation after approval

Conditions

Can delay or produce temporary wins; stronger if paired with political leverage or alternate implementation plan.

Risks

Appeals often reverse trial wins; courts defer to agency environmental review in many cases.
